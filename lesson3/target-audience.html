Background:

She believes in the power of networking and seeks opportunities to connect with fellow students and mentors who can guide her through her academic journey.

Motivations and Needs:

Access to a platform that provides valuable educational resources and study materials specific to her courses and subjects Opportunities for career development and part-time job listings relevant to her field of study Connecting with fellow students to collaborate on group projects and exchange study notes Discovering academic events, seminars, and workshops happening in Madrid Access to a database of tutors or mentors who can provide guidance and support in her academic pursuits

Difficulty finding reliable and up-to-date study materials for her courses Limited knowledge about available part-time job opportunities in Madrid Feeling isolated and lacking a sense of community among her peers Overwhelmed by the sheer number of academic events happening in Madrid and struggling to filter out the most relevant ones Unsure of where to find trust, worthy and experienced tutors or mentors in her area of study

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Study Material Discovery

Emma is preparing for her upcoming Economics exam but is struggling to find relevant study materials. She visits your website and discovers a dedicated section where she can access curated study resources, lecture notes, and practice exams specific to her course. This helps her gain a better understanding of the subject and boosts her confidence for the exam.

Scenario 2: Part-time Job Search

Sara is looking for a part-time job to support her studies. She visits your website and finds a section featuring job listings specifically tailored to students in Madrid. The listings include positions in various fields, including internships, freelance opportunities, and part-time positions. She can filter the listings based on her interests and availability, making her job search more efficient.

Scenario 3: Peer Collaboration

Sara is working on a group project for her Economics course. She visits your website's student community forum, where she can connect with other students from her university and across Madrid. She posts about her project and receives valuable input, suggestions, and resources from her peers. This collaborative environment helps her improve her project and fosters a sense of camaraderie among students.

Scenario 4: Professional Development Workshop

Javier, a passionate teacher, wants to enhance his teaching skills and learn about the latest educational trends. He visits your website and discovers a section dedicated to professional development workshops and conferences happening in Madrid. He finds a workshop on innovative teaching methods and registers for the event. Attending the workshop helps him gain new insights and techniques that he can apply in his classroom.

Scenario 5: Tutoring Services

Javier wants to offer tutoring services outside his regular teaching hours to generate additional income. He creates a profile on your website's tutoring platform, showcasing his expertise in mathematics. Students seeking tutoring in Madrid can browse through the profiles and connect with Javier. This enables him to share his knowledge and provide academic support while earning extra income.
